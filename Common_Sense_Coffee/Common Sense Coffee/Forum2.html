What's Your Go-To Coffee Order?

Hey everyone! As a fellow coffee lover, I'm always interested in trying new things, but sometimes it's hard to stray away from my go-to order. So, I'm curious - what's your go-to coffee order? Personally, I always go for a medium roast drip coffee with a splash of almond milk and a pump of vanilla syrup. It's the perfect balance of bold flavor and sweetness for me. But maybe you have a different favorite? Do you like a classic latte or a bold espresso shot? Do you prefer a certain type of milk or sweetener?
