What's Your Favorite Way to Brew Coffee?

Hey coffee lovers! As someone who frequents coffee shops, I've noticed that there are so many different ways to brew coffee. From pour-over to French press to espresso, everyone seems to have their own preferred method. Personally, I love a good pour-over. There's something about the slow, deliberate process of pouring hot water over the grounds that makes for a really delicious cup of coffee. Plus, it's a great way to really appreciate the flavors and nuances of different roasts. But I know that everyone has their own preferences, so I'm curious - what's your favorite way to brew coffee? Do you prefer the bold, concentrated flavor of espresso, or the rich, smooth taste of a French press? Or maybe you're all about the convenience of a Keurig or drip coffee maker? Whatever your favorite method may be, I'd love to hear about it! Let's geek out over coffee and share our favorite brewing tips and tricks. Who knows, maybe we'll all discover a new favorite brew method along the way.
